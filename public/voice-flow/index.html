Features

Your voice, your machine.
No cloud required.

Hold a hotkey, say what you need, and the text appears wherever your cursor is. That's it.

🎤

Push-to-talk dictation

Hold your hotkey and speak. Release to transcribe. Text gets pasted right where your cursor is, in any app.

🔒

100% local processing

All transcription happens on your machine. No audio is sent anywhere. Your conversations stay yours.

🧠

13 Whisper models

From tiny (instant results) to large-v3 (max accuracy), plus Distil-Whisper for the best speed-to-quality ratio.

⚡

GPU accelerated

Auto-detects your NVIDIA GPU for fast transcription. No GPU? Falls back to CPU automatically.

📝

Transcription history

Every transcription is saved. Copy, delete, or scroll back through everything you've dictated.

⌨️

Customizable hotkeys

Set your own push-to-talk key combo. Toggle hands-free mode when you want to dictate without holding anything.

How it works

Up and running in 30 seconds.

No installer. No account. No setup wizard.

1

Download

Grab VoiceFlow.zip from GitHub. Extract it anywhere.

2

Run

Double-click VoiceFlow.exe. Pick a Whisper model on first launch.

3

Speak

Hold Ctrl + Win and talk. Release and the text appears at your cursor.

4

Paste

That's it. Works in any app. Email, Slack, your code editor, anywhere.
